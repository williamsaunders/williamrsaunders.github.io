About

I am a first year Ph.D. student in the Department of Astronomy at Boston University. I work with Prof. Paul Withers studying planetary atmospheres using stellar occultations.

I received my Bachelors in Physics and Astronomy from the University of Pennsylvania in 2018, where I worked with Prof. Gary Bernstein on trans-Neptunian objects.
